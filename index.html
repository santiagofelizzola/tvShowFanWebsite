Bio

"The Witcher" is a popular television series based on the book series of the same name by Polish author Andrzej Sapkowski. The show is a fantasy drama that first premiered on December 20, 2019, on the streaming platform Netflix. Developed by Lauren Schmidt Hissrich, it quickly gained immense popularity and critical acclaim for its intriguing storytelling, rich world-building, and captivating performances.

Geralt of Rivia

Geralt, portrayed by Henry Cavill, is a monster hunter, known as a Witcher, who possesses superhuman abilities due to alchemical mutations. He roams the land, taking contracts to kill dangerous creatures and monsters plaguing the lives of ordinary people.

Yennefer of Vengerberg

Yennefer, portrayed by Anya Chalotra, is a powerful sorceress with a tragic past. The series explores her journey from being a disfigured and abused young woman to becoming one of the most formidable mages on the continent.

Ciri

Princess Cirilla, known as Ciri and played by Freya Allan, is a young princess with a mysterious gift and a crucial role in the future of the world. As the last surviving member of the royal family of Cintra, she is on the run, seeking refuge and guidance amidst political turmoil and dark forces threatening the land.
